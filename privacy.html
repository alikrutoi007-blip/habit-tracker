Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-13

What we collect

If you use the app in guest mode (the default), nothing is sent to us. All your data stays in your browser's localStorage.

If you sign in to enable cloud sync, we collect:

  • Email address — used only to authenticate you.
  • App data — your habits, plans, journal entries, sleep records. Stored encrypted at rest, accessible only by you.

Where we store it

We use Supabase hosted in the EU region.

Analytics

None. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracker.

Cookies

We use only functional cookies required for authentication (Supabase session token). No advertising or tracking cookies.

Your rights (GDPR)

  • Access: all your data is visible in the app and exportable on request.
  • Deletion: use the "Delete account" button in Settings to permanently delete your cloud data. Local data on your device must be cleared by you (browser settings).
  • Portability: contact us for a JSON export of your data.

Contact

For privacy questions: baroservicellc@gmail.com
